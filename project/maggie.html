MAGGIE

Maggie is a friendly and social cat that loves cuddling, bird watching, grooming and playing with catnip. She is very tidy and clean and prefers a human who will clean her litter box at least once a day. Maggie has lived most of her life as a single cat and prefers it that way. She is not fond of dogs but might do well with another cat if given her own territory. Maggie would work best with children that can be gentle with her.
